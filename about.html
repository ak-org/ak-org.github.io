About the Visualization

The project follows the Martini Glass narrative visualization guidelines. The Home page represents the base of the visualization structure. The arrow leads user through additional pages offering more information about the poverty and disease. The final page shows the interactive chart representing the funnel of the glass. The arrow on the third page represents the jumping point. On the last page, user gets an opportunity to interact, explore and highlight the data. The first three pages represent the author driven content while last page represents the reader driven content.
